Click me for Instructions! The board consists of 6 rows and 7 columns and 42 total spaces on the board.

Each player has at most 21 possible turns to try to win, draw or lose.

You can click the board to place your piece in the column you wish to place it in.

If you get 4 consecutive in a row, column or in a diagonal you win the game. If this does not happen, the game will end up being a tie.
